Letter of Credit (LC)
- Bank guarantee of payment upon compliant document presentation
- Types: sight LC, usance LC, confirmed LC
- Reduces counterparty risk for exporter
- Requires strict document compliance (UCP 600)

Documentary Collection
- Bank acts as intermediary; no payment guarantee
- Lower cost than LC; higher risk for exporter
- D/P (Documents against Payment) vs D/P (Documents against Acceptance)

Export Credit Insurance
- Covers non-payment by foreign buyer
- Provided by export credit agencies (ECAs)
- Recommended for high-risk markets or new buyers
